A GIS data platform supports all stakeholders with rich, shareable data (agro-ecology, water, land, soils, GHG, etc.), respecting the proper protocols of data confidentiality.

Food and Agriculture MIS

The Food and Agriculture MIS is a supporting tool for the Food and Agriculture Initiative, an evidence based for the country-led and country-owned initiative to accelerate SDG1 and SDG 2, using the most sophisticated tools available, including advanced geo-spatial modeling and analytics to identify the biggest opportunities to raise the incomes and reduce the inequities and vulnerabilities of rural populations, who constitute the vast majority of the world's poor.

ABOUT

Food and Agriculture MIS is a repository of data on agriculture food security and nutrition in Pakistan. The data is made available for research and effective policy making. Food and Agriculture MIS is a collaborative project of the Ministry of National Food Security and Research, Pakistan and the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations.
